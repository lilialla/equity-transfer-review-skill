# Materials Checklist

Use this reference when the user provides a data room, corporate archive, scanned documents, OCR output, public registry exports, or historical transaction materials.

## Transaction Snapshot

Capture these facts before analysis:

| Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| Target company | Full registered name, unified social credit code, jurisdiction, company type, business scope. |
| Transferor | Registered shareholder or beneficial owner, identity, authority, marital or internal approval issues. |
| Transferee | Qualification, related-party status, foreign investor status, industry restrictions, funding source if relevant. |
| Subject | Percentage, subscribed capital, paid-in capital, share class, indirect interest, employee platform units. |
| Price | Total price, valuation basis, payment path, tax allocation, escrow or holdback. |
| Dates | Valuation date, signing date, closing date, registration date, long-stop date. |
| Purpose | Exit, control acquisition, employee incentive, restructuring, financing, VIE or offshore holding adjustment. |

## Evidence Map

For every material document, record:

| Field | Requirement |
|---|---|
| File name | Use the actual file name or a stable short label. |
| Document type | Agreement, articles, resolution, payment proof, registry export, tax document, litigation record, approval, correspondence. |
| Date | Signing, issuance, approval, filing, or retrieval date. |
| Parties or issuer | Who signed, sealed, issued, or exported it. |
| Reliability | Original, notarized copy, scanned copy, OCR only, screenshot, platform export, party statement, draft. |
| Key pages | Page, article, image number, or screenshot path. |
| Open issues | Missing signature, unclear stamp, inconsistent entity name, unreadable amount, date conflict, incomplete appendix. |

For scanned or OCR materials, mark readability. Recheck the original image for amounts, percentages, dates, names, seals, signatures, and handwritten changes.

## Core Materials

Request or inspect:

- Current and historical business registration files, articles of association, shareholder register, capital contribution records, annual reports, and change-registration records.
- Current cap table and historical equity changes, including capital increases, transfers, nominee holding, employee platform changes, repurchases, and share pledge releases.
- All shareholder agreements, investment agreements, supplemental agreements, side letters, VAM or repurchase arrangements, preferential rights documents, board or shareholder resolutions, and waiver letters.
- Transfer agreement drafts, payment proof, valuation reports, audit reports, tax filings, transaction tax declarations, and bank records.
- Litigation, enforcement, pledge, freeze, bankruptcy, administrative penalty, material contract, financing, guarantee, and related-party documents.
- Spouse consent, inheritance documents, guardianship or minority capacity documents, bankruptcy administrator consent, or executor documents when natural-person facts indicate a need.
- Industry permits, regulatory approvals, foreign investment documents, SASAC or state-owned asset transaction documents, listed-company or NEEQ disclosures, merger-control materials, cybersecurity or data-export materials when applicable.

## Scenario-Specific Materials

Use this section after classifying the deal with `scenario-gates.md`.

| Scenario | Additional materials |
|---|---|
| Limited-liability company equity transfer | Preemptive-right notices and receipts, other shareholders' written waiver or consent, shareholder meeting resolution, revised articles, shareholder register update, market-regulation filing package. |
| Contribution liability | Subscription schedule, paid-in proof, capital verification report if any, non-cash contribution valuation, historical contribution transfer documents, creditor notices or acceleration materials, contribution defect cure evidence. |
| 股份公司股份转让 | Share register, share certificate or depository record, restricted-share status, lock-up documents, board/shareholder approval, disclosure documents, transfer venue or method confirmation. |
| Partnership or employee platform unit transfer | Partnership agreement, GP consent, platform management rules, incentive plan, vesting and repurchase records, employee consent, tax withholding arrangement, updated partner or platform register. |
| Upper-level holding vehicle transfer | Upstream cap table, offshore or domestic holding documents, investment agreements, change-of-control clauses, downstream consent or no-breach confirmation, indirect transfer tax analysis materials. |
| Offshore or VIE interest transfer | Offshore registry documents, VIE control agreements, ODI/FDI/SAFE materials, foreign investment access analysis, bank pre-review list, tax filing documents, data/cyber materials if relevant. |
| State-owned asset transfer | Internal approval, asset valuation report, appraisal filing or approval, public listing documents, asset exchange transaction documents, local SASAC or competent authority requirements. |
| Listed, NEEQ, or securities-regulated transfer | Lock-up status, disclosure plan, exchange/NEEQ rules check, related-party transaction approval, insider information control record, reduction or transfer compliance confirmation. |
| Tax, foreign exchange, and payment | Tax basis documents, tax declaration forms, withholding evidence, stamp duty proof, bank account and payment route materials, FX registration or bank review documents for cross-border payment. |
| Merger control or competition | Revenue data, control structure before/after, market share materials, filing threshold analysis, no-gun-jumping covenant, clearance or no-filing memo. |

## Public Search Register

When using public or platform sources, record:

- Source name.
- URL or database name.
- Search subject and exact query.
- Retrieval date.
- Screenshot, export, or archive path.
- Result status: found, not found, blocked, inconsistent, or needs official verification.

Use public data as a verification aid, not as a substitute for transaction documents.
