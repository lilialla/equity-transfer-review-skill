# Intake Questionnaire

Use this reference when the user has not provided transaction documents, or when the uploaded materials are too incomplete to start review.

Do not pretend that the deal has been reviewed. Produce an intake pack with targeted questions, priority materials, and a proposed review path.

## Minimum Deal Facts

Ask for these facts first:

| Topic | Questions |
|---|---|
| Target | Full registered name, unified social credit code, jurisdiction, company type, and business scope. |
| Transferor | Registered shareholder or beneficial owner, natural person or entity, state-owned/foreign/listed/regulated status, and authority to transfer. |
| Transferee | Identity, related-party status, foreign investor status, industry qualification, and funding source if relevant. |
| Subject | Percentage, subscribed capital, paid-in capital, share class, partnership unit, employee incentive unit, upper-level vehicle interest, or offshore/VIE interest. |
| Price | Total price, valuation basis, payment account, installment plan, escrow or holdback, tax allocation, and currency. |
| Timing | Signing date, valuation date, target closing date, registration date, and long-stop date. |
| Purpose | Exit, control acquisition, internal restructuring, employee incentive, financing exit, offshore restructuring, or dispute settlement. |

## Gate Questions

Ask these before drafting a review plan:

1. Is the target or either party state-owned, listed, NEEQ-listed, foreign-invested, financial, education, medical, internet, data-heavy, or otherwise licensed?
2. Is the transferred interest direct equity, shares, partnership units, employee incentive units, upper-level holding vehicle interests, offshore interests, or VIE-related rights?
3. Is any capital contribution unpaid, accelerated, defective, non-monetary, disputed, withdrawn, or unsupported by proof?
4. Are there articles, shareholder agreements, investment agreements, side letters, preemptive rights, consent rights, co-sale, drag-along, lock-up, repurchase, VAM, or control-change restrictions?
5. Are pledge, freeze, enforcement, bankruptcy, inheritance, marital property, nominee holding, trust, or beneficial ownership issues involved?
6. Are tax filing, withholding, low-price transfer adjustment, foreign exchange, cross-border payment, or bank review steps expected?
7. Is any approval, filing, exchange listing, disclosure, merger-control filing, national security review, data/cyber review, or third-party consent potentially needed?

## Intake Output

When facts are incomplete, output:

```markdown
## 初步交易画像

## 关键追问

| 优先级 | 问题 | 为什么影响交割 | 回答方式 |
|---|---|---|---|

## 首轮资料清单

| 优先级 | 材料 | 提供方 | 用途 |
|---|---|---|---|

## 建议审核路径

1. 先完成交易类型分流。
2. 再核验权属、出资、转让限制、批准程序和监管闸门。
3. 最后输出红旗、条款修改和交割条件。
```

Keep the questions short and targeted. Do not ask for all possible documents at once if the deal can be triaged with fewer facts.
